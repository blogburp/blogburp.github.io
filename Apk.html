Scanner App


Your all in one solution for snanning a document in an android device


About

This app turns your mobile into a compact scanner, which can be used for scanning handwritten notes and printed documents and converts pdf into documents format.

The following features are explained in the scanner app which makes it somewhat unique.
▪ Scan photos, handwritten or printed documents.
▪ Add Signature in the scanned documents.
▪ PDF manager that compiles all the documents.
▪ Detects page frame and corrects perspective.
▪ Fast and smooth Image Processing on the fly.
▪ Scans are saved to your device as images and PDF.
▪ Detect text using the OCR functionality of the app.
▪ Save the detected texts as editable text files to your device.
▪ Easily share scanned docs with others through social media, email attachments, etc.
